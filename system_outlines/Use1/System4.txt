Intersection Safety Advisor is built upon transformer-based Vision Language Models (VLM) that integrate video data from roadside cameras with textual traffic incident reports for urban road safety analysis. It supports city traffic safety departments by providing comprehensive situational awareness and early warnings about hazardous intersections. The system uses VLM capabilities to correlate visual cues and natural language data, improving the detection of complex safety issues such as near-misses or evolving congestion scenarios that traditional sensors might overlook. It is offered by Novaris Transportation Analytics, a company focused on AI-driven public infrastructure enhancements. The AI subject includes urban drivers, pedestrians, and local enforcement agencies who rely on the system to anticipate and prevent intersection-related accidents.
