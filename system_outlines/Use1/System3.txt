Velocity Alert Network employs an ensemble of Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) and Gradient Boosted Decision Trees (GBDT) to analyze sequential vehicle telemetry and environmental data along highways. Designed for highway traffic management agencies, the system's objective is to promptly detect unusual speed patterns and hazardous driving behaviors to mitigate collision risks. The RNN captures temporal dependencies and driver behavior trends from continuous telemetry streams, while GBDTs incorporate contextual factors such as weather and traffic density to improve risk prediction accuracy. Horizon Road Safety Services, a specialized engineering consultancy, develops and deploys the system. Drivers, highway maintenance crews, and emergency response teams are the key groups directly influenced by the system’s safety alerts and interventions.
