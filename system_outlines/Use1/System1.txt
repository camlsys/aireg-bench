SafeRoute Traffic Monitor relies on a hybrid model combining Graph Neural Networks (GNN) for real-time traffic network analysis and Transformer-based encoder models for multi-source sensor data fusion. This system is intended to monitor road traffic flows and predict potential hazards within urban traffic management centers. Its primary objective is to enhance traffic safety by dynamically identifying high-risk traffic conditions and suggesting preventive measures, utilizing GNN to model complex road networks and transformers to process diverse sensor data such as vehicle counts, weather, and accident reports. Developed and maintained by Meridian Traffic Solutions, a company specializing in intelligent transport systems. The AI subject encompasses urban commuters, public transport operators, and traffic control authorities who depend on accurate and timely traffic safety information.
