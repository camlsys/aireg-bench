The system named Gas Safety Insight utilizes a hybrid approach based on Gradient Boosted Decision Trees (GBDT) combined with encoder-only Transformers for processing sensor data and real-time operational logs. It is intended for continuous monitoring and real-time anomaly detection within the natural gas supply network to prevent leaks, overpressure, or system failures. The system’s primary objective is to ensure operational safety by predicting potential safety hazards before they manifest, leveraging sensor fusion, temporal pattern recognition, and contextual data encoding. Gas Safety Insight is developed and maintained by Norwin Industrial Technologies, a specialist provider in critical infrastructure AI solutions. The directly affected parties are gas network operators, maintenance personnel, and ultimately the end-users relying on safe natural gas supply.
