The system called Pipeline Safety Guardian is based exclusively on Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) processing time-series pressure and flow sensor data supplemented by traditional Random Forest classifiers for fault classification. It serves the gas distribution sector by identifying anomalies in pipeline integrity and early detection of potentially hazardous conditions such as cracks or pressure drops. Its key capability is rapid interpretation and classification of multi-sensor input streams to trigger alerts and safety interventions, aiming to minimize risk to property and human health. Pipeline Safety Guardian is produced and distributed by Meridian Safety Systems, an industrial AI solutions provider focused on energy infrastructure. The AI subject includes pipeline operators, field technicians, and communities located near gas distribution routes.
