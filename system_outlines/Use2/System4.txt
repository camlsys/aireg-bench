Sentinel Control Platform employs a transformer-based encoder-decoder model tailored for processing heterogeneous time-series data from gas supply control systems alongside reinforcement learning algorithms to optimize safety control policies. It supports gas supply operators in mitigating dangerous scenarios by continuously learning from operational feedback and adapting control signals for valves and regulators. Its technological capabilities include predictive modeling of system states and decision-making under uncertainty, ensuring rapid response to emergent safety threats. Sentinel Control Platform is developed and offered by Arched Systems Corporation, a provider of industrial AI automation and safety software solutions. The persons directly affected by this system are control room operators, maintenance staff, and communities dependent on safe, uninterrupted gas service.
