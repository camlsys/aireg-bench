Distributed Sensor Secure uses a Graph Neural Network (GNN)-based architecture combined with transformer encoders to model relationships and interactions between distributed gas sensors across a wide-area transmission network. It is designed to ensure safety in high-pressure gas transmission by dynamically assessing the network state and predicting failure propagation paths. The system’s objective is to provide enhanced situational awareness and risk mitigation through graph-based spatial correlation and temporal analysis of sensor data under varying operational conditions. Distributed Sensor Secure is developed by Vectron Energy Analytics, a company specializing in AI-enabled safety systems for utilities. The AI subject impacted by this system comprises pipeline engineers, network control centers, and ultimately residents in service areas.
