Name and Type: Recruitment Decision Forest, utilizing a Gradient Boosted Decision Tree (GBDT) ensemble geared towards structured data analysis including candidate metadata, application form responses, and historical hiring outcomes. System use: Applied within enterprise recruitment divisions for initial candidate screening and scoring based on quantitative and categorical features to support targeted advertising and shortlisting processes. Objective and Technological Capabilities: This system aims to deliver transparent, interpretable candidate rankings by learning from past recruitment data patterns, enabling recruiters to focus on high-potential profiles while optimizing job ad placements with data-driven insights. Provider: Created by Meridian Analytics Solutions, a provider of enterprise AI software tailored for workforce optimization. AI subject: Applicants whose data profiles are used for scoring and candidates who experience selection results influenced by the model’s screening, as well as recruiters utilizing the system’s insights.
