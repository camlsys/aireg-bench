Name and Type: Candidate Language Matcher, leveraging an encoder-only transformer model specialized in semantic similarity analysis between job adverts and application texts in multiple languages. System use: Intended for global job platforms and multinational corporations to improve the relevance of job advertisements shown to diverse applicant pools and to refine the filtering of multilingual applications. Objective and Technological Capabilities: It facilitates enhanced matching by semantically encoding both job posting language and candidate input to assess fit beyond keyword matches, supporting fairness and accuracy in diverse linguistic and cultural contexts. Provider: Offered by Veridico Workforce Technologies, a multinational company developing AI-aided HR tools for international recruitment challenges. AI subject: Multilingual job seekers and recruitment officers who receive and evaluate applications influenced by the system’s semantic relevance scoring.
