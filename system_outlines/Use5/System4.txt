Name and Type: Application Filter Network, based on a convolutional neural network (CNN) adapted for structured and lightly preprocessed textual data from candidate resumes and cover letters. System use: Employed by mid-sized recruitment agencies and online job portals to automatically classify and prioritize applications for targeted role advertising and candidate shortlisting. Objective and Technological Capabilities: The system reduces manual workload by detecting key feature patterns indicative of candidate suitability through layered convolutional feature extraction, enabling efficient large-volume application processing. Provider: Developed by Horizon Talent Solutions, a software firm dedicated to AI products for mid-market HR service providers. AI subject: Candidates submitting job applications and recruitment professionals who rely on this system for faster, automated filtering and job advertisement targeting.
