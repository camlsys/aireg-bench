Name and Type: Talent Insight Model, based primarily on a transformer-based encoder-decoder architecture fine-tuned for natural language processing of resumes and job descriptions. System use: Designed to assist recruitment firms and corporate HR departments in filtering and ranking job applications while matching candidates with targeted job advertisements. Objective and Technological Capabilities: The system’s main goal is to improve recruitment efficiency by accurately extracting relevant skills, experience, and qualifications from unstructured text and aligning these with job requirements using contextual embeddings and relevance scoring. Provider: Developed and maintained by Sterling Recruitment Technologies, a global HR solutions provider specializing in AI-driven talent acquisition. AI subject: Job applicants and hiring managers who interact with the recruitment platform and rely on automated filtering and personalized job matching outcomes.
