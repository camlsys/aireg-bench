Workforce Transition Advisor employs an encoder-only transformer model trained on employment history data and associated textual performance reviews to facilitate decision-making in employment contract cessation. Intended for use in organizational HR departments and labor consultancy firms, this system aims to provide interpretable risk assessment and fairness guidance in labor contract termination scenarios. Its technological strengths include contextual embedding of multidimensional employee data and natural language understanding to highlight potential legal and ethical issues in termination proposals. This system is provided by Novus Employment Analytics, a provider specializing in AI-driven HR decision support systems, with the workforce members under evaluation as the directly impacted subjects.
