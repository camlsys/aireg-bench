Legal Termination Assessment Framework utilizes a combination of gradient-boosted decision trees (GBDT) and transformer-based natural language processing models to analyze employee records and contract-related documentation. It is designed specifically for the human resources sector to support fair and compliant decisions regarding work contract terminations. The system’s primary objective is to evaluate eligibility and risks associated with terminating contractual relationships by combining structured employee data analysis and contextual interpretation of legal text through its dual-model approach. Developed and marketed by Meridian Labor Solutions, a specialist in workforce management AI technologies, this system is intended for employees subject to contract termination processes.
