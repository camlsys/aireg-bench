Employment Status Evaluator is built on a hybrid model combining support vector machines (SVM) for structured tabular employment data classification and transformer-based text encoders for analyzing contractual clauses and employee communications. The system targets the industrial and service sectors, focusing on objectively supporting management decisions about ending work-related contractual relationships. Its objective is to offer balanced, evidence-based termination recommendations by fusing quantitative employment indicators and qualitative text analysis within a robust, interpretable framework. The system is developed and distributed by Apex Workforce Solutions, an enterprise dedicated to advanced HR analytics tools, affecting employees potentially facing contract termination.
