Contractual Separation Insight relies on an ensemble of random forest algorithms integrated with large language models (LLMs) focused on policy interpretation and behavioral analytics. It serves the corporate HR and compliance sectors, delivering decision support for terminating employee contracts while ensuring adherence to company policies and labor laws. The system’s key capability is to synthesize quantitative performance metrics with qualitative policy text analysis, enabling predictive outcome modeling and transparent recommendation generation. Created by Vanguard Human Capital Technologies, Contractual Separation Insight targets workers whose employment status may be affected by contractual termination decisions.
