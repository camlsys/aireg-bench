Consumer Credit Transformer, utilizing an encoder-only transformer model tailored for tabular data interpretation. Intended for the personal lending sector, this system evaluates credit risk by capturing complex, non-linear relationships across multimodal financial records, transaction histories, and customer metadata. It achieves this through self-attention mechanisms that improve prediction accuracy and robustness on diverse datasets. Developed and distributed by Veritas Risk Solutions, a technology provider specializing in AI-driven financial decision support tools, the AI subjects are natural persons applying for credit products who experience outcomes based on the system’s creditworthiness assessments.
