Credit Evaluation Network, based on Gradient Boosted Decision Trees (GBDT). This AI system is designed to assess the creditworthiness of natural persons within the consumer finance sector. Its primary objective is to deliver interpretable and accurate credit scores by leveraging GBDT’s ability to handle structured financial and demographic data, enabling nuanced risk stratification and prediction of default likelihood. The system is developed and maintained by Meridian Financial Analytics, a specialist firm providing advanced credit risk solutions to banking institutions. The AI subjects directly affected are individual loan applicants and credit consumers whose credit scores influence lending decisions.
