Personal Credit Score Predictor, based on a multilayer perceptron (MLP) neural network designed for tabular financial and personal data inputs. Targeted at microfinance institutions and retail lenders, the system aims to compute reliable credit scores by learning complex feature interactions within limited or sparse data environments commonly found in emerging markets. Its feedforward architecture facilitates fast inference and adaptability to evolving credit criteria. The system is provided by Ascendant Credit Technologies, an enterprise focused on scalable AI credit scoring solutions for underserved populations. The AI subjects are natural persons in need of credit, including those with limited credit history, whose financial access decisions depend on the system outputs.
