Creditworthiness Random Forest Framework, employing Random Forest classifiers to generate comprehensive credit risk profiles for individuals in consumer banking. The system’s objective is to provide efficient, explainable credit scoring by aggregating decisions from multiple decision trees trained on large volumes of credit bureau data and behavioral attributes. This ensemble learning approach supports consistency and transparency required by regulatory standards. It is produced by Crestview Analytics, a data science company delivering credit scoring platforms to financial services companies. The AI subjects are consumers and borrowers evaluated for personal loan eligibility and credit line adjustments.
