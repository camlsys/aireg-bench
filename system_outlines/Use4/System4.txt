Test Guardian Observer features a decoder-only Transformer model adapted for real-time natural language processing of textual chat logs and written notes from digital exam platforms, focusing on detecting collusive or deceptive textual interactions among students during assessments. Serving educational sectors employing remote or hybrid testing modes, the system’s core functionality centers on semantic analysis and pattern detection to identify suspicious message exchanges while excluding personal biometric data. This enables institutions to maintain academic integrity through automatic moderation and alert generation based on contextual behavioral indicators. The system is provided by Phoenix Academic Integrity Services, a technology provider specializing in AI-driven monitoring tools for modern educational contexts. The AI subjects affected include students participating in digitally proctored exams, whose communication is analyzed under predefined ethical limits to prevent misconduct.
