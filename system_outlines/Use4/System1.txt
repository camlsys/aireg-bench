Insight Proctor Analytics utilizes a combination of transformer-based Vision Language Models (VLM) to analyze video feeds and contextual exam content in real-time for monitoring student behavior during tests within academic institutions. The system is designed to detect prohibited behaviors such as unauthorized communication, use of forbidden materials, or suspicious gestures by cross-referencing visual cues with test metadata in educational testing environments. Its objective is to provide accurate, transparent, and non-intrusive detection of anomalies leveraging state-of-the-art visual and semantic pattern recognition to support exam integrity while respecting privacy constraints. Insight Proctor Analytics is developed and distributed by Meridian Educational Technologies, a global provider of intelligent learning and assessment solutions. The AI subject comprises students enrolled in supervised examinations who are monitored to uphold fair testing standards and equitable academic outcomes.
