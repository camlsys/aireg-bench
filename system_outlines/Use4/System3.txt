Examination Integrity Sentinel relies on Support Vector Machines (SVM) applied to tabular data capturing digital interaction logs and environmental sensor inputs to identify patterns associated with prohibited test behaviors in schools and universities. Intended to support proctoring frameworks, this system processes structured data inputs such as software usage, device activity, and ambient noise levels to flag potential violations through interpretable decision boundaries. Its streamlined algorithmic foundation facilitates real-time evaluation and clear audit trails for institutional review while adhering to ethical data usage principles. The product is developed and marketed by Altair Educational Solutions, an international firm providing advanced assessment monitoring technologies. The AI subject group includes test-taking students monitored to ensure compliance with exam rules in both physical and computer-based settings.
