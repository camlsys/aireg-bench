Priority Response Analytics, based on a Gradient Boosted Decision Tree (GBDT) model for structured incident data and a Transformer encoder for processing textual dispatch notes, is designed to prioritize emergency calls for police, fire, and medical services by combining numerical and unstructured data inputs. This system serves emergency dispatch centers providing real-time prioritization of incidents to optimize resource allocation and response times. Its primary objective is to accurately assess the urgency and severity of emergency situations using integrated data analysis and natural language understanding, enabling dispatchers to make informed, data-driven priority decisions. Priority Response Analytics is developed and maintained by Sentinel Technologies, a specialist provider of AI-driven public safety solutions. The directly affected individuals include emergency responders, dispatch operators, and ultimately the public requiring emergency assistance.
