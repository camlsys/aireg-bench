Emergency Dispatch Prioritization Engine employs a hybrid neural network combining a Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) for geographic and sensor-image data analysis with a Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) RNN model for temporal event sequence prediction to establish dispatch priority in public safety operations. It is intended for use by municipal police, fire departments, and emergency medical services to enhance rapid response through multimodal data fusion. The system’s core capability lies in its ability to interpret spatial data alongside chronological patterns in past incidents, providing dynamic prioritization recommendations tailored to evolving emergency scenarios. The provider of this system is Urban Safety Analytics, a company focused on AI applications for smart city emergency infrastructures. The AI subject consists primarily of emergency response teams and dispatch personnel whose operational decisions it supports.
