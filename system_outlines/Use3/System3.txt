Outcome Insight Engine based on convolutional neural networks (CNN) combined with transformer-based language models to analyze both visual learning artifacts (e.g., handwritten assignments, diagrams) and textual responses in formal education settings. This system serves primary and secondary schools by automatically grading and evaluating multimodal student submissions to guide individualized remediation and enrichment strategies. Its objective is to enhance the reliability and consistency of assessment by integrating visual pattern recognition with deep contextual language understanding, facilitating nuanced evaluation beyond traditional grading. The system is produced by Sterling Educational Systems, a provider specializing in innovative AI platforms for classroom assessment enhancement. The AI subjects include students whose work is evaluated and educators who utilize the outcome analyses to tailor instructional support.
