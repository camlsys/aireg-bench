Competency Evaluation Framework utilizing gradient boosted decision trees (GBDT) trained on structured performance metrics and learner interaction logs. Intended for vocational training centers and lifelong learning programs, it assesses skill acquisition and mastery levels across diverse technical and practical competencies. The system’s objective is to provide accurate, interpretable competency scores that support adaptive curriculum steering and certification readiness, leveraging the GBDT’s strength in handling heterogeneous tabular data and feature importance explanations. Developed by Horizon Learning Analytics, an education technology company providing enterprise-grade AI tools for workforce development and institutional learning quality assurance. The AI subjects are trainees and instructors within vocational and adult education programs who rely on the system to measure progress and inform instructional adjustments.
