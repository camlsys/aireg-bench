Adaptive Learning Outcome Analyzer relying on transformer-based encoder-decoder models specialized in text and numerical data processing. This system is designed for evaluating student assessment results and real-time learning progress in primary, secondary, and higher education institutions. Its primary objective is to provide detailed analysis of learning outcomes, detect knowledge gaps, and generate personalized feedback and learning path recommendations using advanced natural language understanding and tabular data interpretation. The system is developed and maintained by Meridian Cognition Technologies, a specialist provider focusing on AI solutions for educational assessment. The AI subjects are learners and educators who engage with the system’s insights to adjust teaching strategies and learning activities.
