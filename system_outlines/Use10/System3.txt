Voter Response Simulator incorporates recurrent neural networks (RNN) combined with gradient-boosted decision trees (GBDT) to model and predict individual voter reactions to various political messaging stimuli during referenda campaigns, operating within the political consultancy sector. The system’s objective is to simulate and optimize communication strategies by predicting which messages are most likely to influence voter behavior based on historical response data and real-time feedback loops, allowing campaigners to tailor outreach effectively. Developed by Parallax Strategy Solutions, a firm providing AI-driven voter engagement and campaign tools. The AI subjects are registered voters who receive campaign communications influenced indirectly through the system’s predictions and message optimization.
