Election Sentiment Transformer employs encoder-only transformer architectures optimized for real-time social media text analysis to monitor, predict, and influence public sentiment regarding political candidates and referenda in democratic electoral processes. Its principal capability lies in ingesting vast streams of social media data to identify trending opinions and generate strategic counter-messages or reinforcing narratives that can sway public discourse and individual voter sentiment. The system is produced and maintained by Horizon Analytics Group, a data intelligence company focused on democratic engagement technologies. Voters engaging with social media platforms and exposed to the system-generated content constitute the AI subjects, experiencing potential shifts in electoral sentiment.
