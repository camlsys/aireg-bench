Court Facts Synthesizer deploys a combined approach with recurrent neural networks (RNN) for temporal sequence modeling of case events and a random forest algorithm for extracting and weighing evidentiary elements. It serves judicial bodies needing structured chronological analysis and fact synthesis to underpin legal reasoning linked to evolving case narratives. The system’s core capability lies in converting unstructured case information into coherent timelines and predictive outcome indicators based on historical judicial decisions. Developed by Juris Data Solutions, a legal technology firm specializing in judicial workflow enhancement, the system is supplied through governmental procurement channels. The individuals directly affected by this AI system include judges and prosecutorial staff who benefit from enhanced clarity and consistency in fact application within case adjudication.
