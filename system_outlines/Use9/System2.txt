Legal Context Navigator utilizes an encoder-only transformer-based large language model trained on a diverse corpus of statutory, regulatory, and case law texts to support judicial fact-finding and law application. The system targets judicial authorities by facilitating nuanced semantic interpretation of legal texts and automatically mapping relevant laws to complex fact scenarios. Its technology enables dynamic querying of legislative documents, contextual summarization, and tailored retrieval of precedent cases with high precision in matching legal criteria. Lexicon Analytics Corporation designs and deploys this technology, providing direct operational support to judicial bodies under governmental frameworks. The AI subject includes judges and their legal assistants who apply this system to ensure thoroughness and consistency in judicial reasoning and decision-making.
