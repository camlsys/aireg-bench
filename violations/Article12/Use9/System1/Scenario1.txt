[a] **Quotation:**  
"High-risk AI systems shall technically allow for the automatic recording of events (logs) over the lifetime of the system."

[b] **Guideline:**  
The system must implement continuous, automated event logging from deployment until decommissioning, capturing relevant runtime data without requiring manual activation or intervention to ensure a complete audit trail.

[c] **Violation:**  
Judicial Insight Assistant’s logging module only activates upon manual triggers initiated by system administrators during specific maintenance windows, resulting in gaps in log data during normal operation periods.

[d] **Justification:**  
This violation subtly breaches the requirement because it implies incomplete lifetime logs due to manual control, undermining automated record-keeping. It is realistic as companies might offload logging control to reduce data volume or overhead, inadvertently contravening the mandatory automatic logging obligation.
