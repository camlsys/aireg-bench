[a] **Quotation:**  
"Logging capabilities shall enable the recording of events relevant for facilitating the post-market monitoring referred to in Article 72."  

[b] **Guideline:**  
Logs must record granular, timestamped predictions, input feature values, and contextual metadata that allow detailed performance analysis and error tracking in post-market monitoring to detect unintended consequences or degradation over time.  

[c] **Violation:**  
The system logs anonymized credit scores without linking them to specific input features or contextual factors such as applicant demographics or economic conditions, preventing detailed post-market performance analysis or identification of subgroup biases.  

[d] **Justification:**  
By anonymizing or aggregating logged outputs without sufficient linkage to inputs, the system limits the ability to perform root-cause analyses and monitor fairness and accuracy trends over time, undermining the post-market monitoring process in a subtle manner that could easily be overlooked as a privacy or data protection measure.
