[a] **Quotation:**  
"Logging capabilities shall enable ... facilitating the post-market monitoring ... and monitoring the operation of high-risk AI systems."  

[b] **Guideline:**  
Logs should consistently record operational metrics, adjustment parameters, and data input variations to allow continuous performance assessment, degradation detection, and support periodic reviews post-deployment.  

[c] **Violation:**  
Gas Safety Insight does not log changes to data preprocessing configurations or model hyperparameters applied in real-time, hindering the ability to monitor how ongoing adjustments affect system performance after deployment.  

[d] **Justification:**  
Neglecting to capture configuration changes obscures important factors driving operational behavior and prevents effective post-market surveillance. This violation is subtle because it relates to internal system maintenance data often assumed non-essential for logging.
