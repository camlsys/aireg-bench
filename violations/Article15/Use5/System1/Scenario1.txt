After deployment, the Talent Insight Model undergoes periodic fine-tuning using new recruitment data without comprehensive validation, leading to gradual degradation in candidate ranking accuracy for certain industries, which is not detected or corrected until several recruitment cycles have passed.  

[d] **Justification:**
